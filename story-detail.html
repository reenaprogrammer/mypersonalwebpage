My Journey: One Line of Code at a Time ☕

At 31, I experienced a profound awakening. I realized I wanted to do something truly meaningful and become the person I was always meant to be. While it was natural for many to criticize my life choices at this stage, I held onto a simple truth: we become what we think about. I knew that through relentless hard work, I could eventually define my own success.

Driven by that belief, I did exactly what others told me I couldn't do. I may not be perfect, and I may not be a master yet, but I am developing my skills one line of code at a time. I have learned that while the road to mastery takes time, it is truly never too late to start a new chapter.

To make this dream a reality, I took a calculated risk. I left a stable, decent-earning job to enroll in college via distance learning. It hasn't been easy, and the challenges are real, but my determination to build something significant out of this sacrifice keeps me moving forward every day.

This website is a result of that journey. Here we are, pushing into what once seemed like impossibilities, fueled by the hope of achieving something very possible: the chance to truly "live" through my passion.

So yes as said, "It is never too late to be what you might have been." — George Eliot..
